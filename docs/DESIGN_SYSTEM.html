Design Principles

🎯 Bold & Vibrant

Use bright, modern colors and gradients to create energy and appeal to younger demographics who expect visual excitement.

✨ Modern & Clean

Rounded corners, subtle shadows, and ample whitespace create a contemporary feel that feels trustworthy and approachable.

📱 Mobile-First

Design for mobile devices first. Most of our audience browses on phones, so responsive, touch-friendly design is essential.

♿ Accessible

WCAG 2.1 AA compliance. Color contrast ratios meet accessibility standards. Text alternatives for images. Keyboard navigation.

⚡ Performance

Fast load times, optimized images, efficient CSS. Young users expect snappy performance or they'll leave.

🎨 Consistent

Every element follows the design system. Predictability builds trust and makes the site easier to navigate.

Accessibility Guidelines

Color Contrast: All text meets WCAG AA standard (4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text).
Typography: Minimum 16px for body text on mobile. Line height 1.5 minimum for readability.
Focus States: Clear focus indicators on all interactive elements. Minimum 2px outline with sufficient contrast.
Touch Targets: Minimum 44x44px touch targets for buttons and interactive elements.
Images: All images have meaningful alt text. Decorative images use empty alt attributes.
Motion: Animations respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. No auto-playing media with sound.

Brand Voice & Tone

Who We Are

  • Informative: We provide factual, well-researched information on sexual health
  • Approachable: We speak in accessible language, not medical jargon
  • Inclusive: We center diverse experiences and identities
  • Empowering: We help people make informed decisions about their health and rights
  • Contemporary: We feel modern and relevant to young adults
  • Trustworthy: We source reputable news outlets and fact-check rigorously
